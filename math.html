These are short notes about interesting topics in geometry.

  • The Hagge Circle The proof of the Hagge circle is one of my favorites, stringing together one nice observation after another. I also present a special case: the Fuhrmann circle, which unifies the orthocenter, circumcenter, centroid, incenter, Nagel point, nine-point center, and Spieker center.

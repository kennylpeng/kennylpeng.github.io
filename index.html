Kenny Peng

I am a fourth-year computer science PhD student at Cornell, where I am fortunate to be advised by Nikhil Garg and Jon Kleinberg. Previously, I completed my undergraduate degree in mathematics at Princeton, where I was lucky to work closely with Arvind Narayanan and Evita Nestoridi.

I am based at Cornell Tech in New York City, where I am a member of Cornell's AI, Policy, and Practice (AIPP) working group. In Summer 2024, I was a PiTech fellow, working on the Restorative Record.

I'm interested in using AI to understand and improve societal systems (like social media, hiring, and school matching). I'm also interested theoretical aspects of matching markets and recommender systems.
